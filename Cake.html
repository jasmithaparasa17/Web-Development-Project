Ingredients

  • ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cups granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ teaspoons baking powder,baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • Fresh strawberries and mint for garnish

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dry Ingredients

    2. In a large mixing bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

  2. Mix the Wet Ingredients

    3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract.

  3. Combine the Wet and Dry Ingredients

    4. Gradually add the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix well. Then slowly pour in the boiling water while stirring the mixture. The batter will be thin, which is normal.

  4. Pour Batter into Cake Pans

    5. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans. Pour the batter evenly between the two pans.

  5. Bake the Cake

    6. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake the cakes for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow the cakes to cool for 10 minutes, then remove them from the pans and transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.

  6. Prepare the Chocolate Ganache

    7. For the topping, you can make a simple chocolate ganache. Heat 1 cup of heavy cream and pour it over 8 oz of chopped dark chocolate. Stir until smooth.

  7. Assemble the Cake

    8. Place one layer of the cake on a serving plate. Spread a layer of ganache and top it with fresh strawberries. Place the second layer on top and pour more ganache over the cake, letting it drip down the sides.

  8. Garnish and Serve

    9. Garnish with more fresh strawberries and mint leaves for an elegant presentation.

Enjoy Your Chocolate Cake!

This is a delightful cake for any occasion, and it pairs perfectly with a hot cup of coffee or tea. Feel free to get creative with your toppings and decorations!
